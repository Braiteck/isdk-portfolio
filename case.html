Cargobooking365
Air Cargo Booking
SAAS-Platform
2021
Website, Custom Development
Air cargo transport made simple
We designed and developed web cargo booking service using an open SaaS-platform to attract new partners – both end customers of cargo services and airlines and their agents providing transportation services
You can choose the desired point of departure and destination, including airports nearby and specify the parameters of each cargo
As easy as buying
a ticket
The service will provide you with a list of airlines and flights with current prices for your cargo. You can choose the best option and send a booking request.
Deal with paperwork
in seconds
The service can generate air waybills in PDF or FWB and FHL messages in only a few clicks.
Be in control of every process
Personal workspace allows you to manage bookings and requests history, organization info, payment methods and track cargos.
